VARSITY 2017

Varsity is an annual sporting series between Bournemouth University and Southampton Solent University and I was asked to photograph portraits of football, basketball, cricket and hockey players. These images are used for official posters for Varsity 2017.

FOLKLORE

The idea behind my project represents my roots. It is about the tradition in my country held for centuries, and that tradition is folklore. My purpose is to introduce folklore to foreign audiences and show it in all its splendour.
