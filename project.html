INTERVIEW



This interview is with Richard Bradbury
who has been a professional photographer for nearly 30 years.
I interview Richard to discover what it is really like to be a photographer. I dig deeper
looking at the advantages and disadvantages of photography industry
and I also ask about the importance of technical skills.

Richard shoots for advertising agencies, magazines
and some direct clients throughout the world. He has shot various campaigns
for big companies such us Sony PlayStation, Tesco, Samsung and M&Ms amongst many others.
His work for The Guinness World Records has enabled him to photograph
extraordinary people across the globe.

MPA Creative & Commercial Photographer of the Year
SWPP Commercial & Advertising Photographer of the Year
AOP Editorial Photographer of the Year
