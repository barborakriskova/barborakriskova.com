DOCUMENTARY FILM

As a student studying media degree,
I was asked to produce a short non-fiction documentary film
with a main theme "Good Deeds". I collaborated with four other MCP students:
Catarina, Mariana, Rebekah and Tom. We split our roles and filmed together as one well-organised
film crew. After the shoot, each of us worked individually
to edit the footage we have made.
